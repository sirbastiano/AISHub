What is AISHub?

AISHub is a structured AIS data platform that organizes global vessel movement observations into daily, consistent datasets. It simplifies development and analysis for teams needing reliable marine traffic information in Earth Observation workflows.

Why it matters

ESA-linked EO and maritime innovation benefits from timely vessel context. The platform can support risk detection, compliance monitoring, and logistics optimization with transparent, consistent AIS inputs.

  • Integrate maritime anomaly detection with satellite-derived alerts.
  • Track route efficiencies and congestion around ports.
  • Correlate vessel tracks with climate and surface observation products.
